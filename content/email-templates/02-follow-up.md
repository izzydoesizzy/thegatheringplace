# Email Template 02 — Follow-Up / After Initial Interest

**Use when:** Someone responded positively to the first touch but hasn't booked a call yet, OR after a brief first exchange where they expressed interest. This deepens the picture.

**Tone:** More substance. Still personal. Starting to paint the vision.

---

**Subject:** A little more context on what I'm building

---

Hey [Name],

Glad it landed. Here's a bit more.

The Gathering Place is built around a simple observation: creative people spend enormous energy showing up the way other people need them to show up. On stage, on calls, on brand. And there's almost nowhere in the professional world where they can just *be* — weird, mid-thought, not-yet-figured-out — without it costing them something.

I want to build that somewhere.

The model:
- A rotating set of high-end properties (Toronto base, a cottage in the Kawarthas, Barbados in the winter, eventually a place in Cape Breton on a cliff overlooking the ocean)
- A professional video studio at every location — no more booking random studios or filming on your phone
- A small group of people — speakers, coaches, writers, designers — who you don't have to explain yourself to
- Access to me, for whatever that's worth, when you need it

The founding cohort is 8-12 people. Membership is ~$2,500/month. The pitch I make to everyone: could you generate $20-25K in new work or revenue over the year if you had access to a world-class space, a pro studio, and a room full of people at this level? Most people I ask that question to say yes immediately.

There's also a sub-track called The Green Room — specifically for professional speakers. I'm not advertising it publicly. If it's as good as I think it'll be, it'll develop a waitlist on its own.

Would love to get 30 minutes on the calendar and hear how it lands for you in person.

Drew
