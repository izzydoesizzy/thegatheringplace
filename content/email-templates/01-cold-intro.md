# Email Template 01 — Cold Intro / First Touch

**Use when:** Reaching out to someone for the first time about The Gathering Place. They may know Drew, but they haven't heard about this yet.

**Tone:** Personal, warm, curious. Not a pitch. An invitation to a conversation.

---

**Subject:** Something I've been building

---

Hey [Name],

I've been sitting on this for a few months — testing it quietly before saying anything publicly — and I think you're one of the people I want to hear it from.

I'm building something called The Gathering Place.

The short version: a private, rotating membership club for creative professionals. Speakers, coaches, writers, designers — people who spend a lot of time being "on" for others and almost no time in a room where they don't have to be. High-end spaces, a professional video studio built in, and a small group of people who get it without needing an explanation.

It's not a mastermind. It's not a conference. The closest thing I can compare it to is a year-long gathering — unstructured enough to let real things happen, curated enough that nobody's wasting anyone's time.

I'm hand-picking the founding cohort. 8-12 people. I've been thinking about you for a while.

No pitch on this call — just want to know if the idea lands with you the way it's been landing in my head.

Are you open to a 20-minute conversation?

Drew

---

*[Note for Drew: Swap "a 20-minute conversation" for a Calendly link or a specific day/time if you want to reduce friction.]*
