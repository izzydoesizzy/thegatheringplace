# Email Template 03 — Post-Conversation / After a Call

**Use when:** You've spoken with someone, the conversation went well, and you want to send them the full picture with a clear next step.

**Tone:** Warm, direct, forward-moving. The conversation happened — now you're formalizing.

---

**Subject:** Great talking — here's everything in one place

---

Hey [Name],

Really enjoyed talking through this with you. Exactly the kind of conversation I built The Gathering Place to have more of.

I've attached the full membership prospectus — everything we covered, plus the location calendar, what's included, and how The Green Room works. Read it at whatever pace makes sense.

The short version of where things stand:

- Founding cohort is 8-12 people. A few seats are already spoken for.
- Membership is ~$2,500/month, terms completely flexible — I'd rather have the right person than fill a seat.
- The pilot week (Forest Hill, Toronto, Feb 15-20) is already in motion — quiet, invite-only, no press. If you want to come see what it actually feels like before committing to anything, that's on the table.

No pressure on timeline. I'm hand-picking this group, not filling a funnel. But if it's sitting right with you, I'd love to know sooner rather than later — not because of scarcity, but because the people in the founding cohort will shape what this becomes.

If you have questions after reading, I'm an email away. And if you want another conversation, just say the word.

Drew

drew@dayoneleadership.com

- `the-gathering-place-prospectus.pdf`
