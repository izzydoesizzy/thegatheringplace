# The Gathering Place — Membership Benefits

## What's Included

---

### Physical Access to the Space

Members can stay at any of the rotating properties throughout the year. Book a room, use it as an office, host client meetings in the space. The properties are high-end, thoughtfully chosen, and available to members when they need them — not just when a retreat is scheduled.

This isn't a perk. For a lot of members, having access to a beautiful, distraction-free space outside their home or office is worth the cost of membership on its own.

---

### Professional Video Studio

An on-site, professionally managed video studio is included in every location. Keynotes, presentations, social content, course material — all of it can be filmed properly, with lighting, sound, and setup handled by the studio partner Drew has already arranged.

No more booking studios in random cities or filming on your phone against a blank wall. You'll leave every visit with usable content.

---

### Social Media Production

Content creation is built into the studio partnership. Members who want to leave a visit with finished social content — not just raw footage — have access to production support as part of their membership. Show up with ideas; leave with posts.

---

### Use the Space for Your Own Retreats

Members can bring up to 3 guests for their own programs, workshops, or retreats — and charge those guests directly. Drew's thinking: if you run a $2,000/head retreat with 3 people, you've covered your monthly membership fee in a single visit.

The space becomes a revenue tool, not just a cost.

---

### Drew — If You Come to Him

Drew will be there. He's not delivering a weekly curriculum. He's not on a schedule. But he's around — for speaker coaching, idea-bouncing, fire-side conversations, and the kind of feedback that can only come from someone who's done 1,000 of these.

The condition: you come to him. He's not a service; he's a presence. Members who use him well tend to get a lot out of it.

---

### 5 Questions on Camera Each Visit

Each visit, members answer 5 brief questions on camera. Thoughtful, short — Drew's style. These answers become part of The Gathering Place's social content.

Members know this going in. It's not a transaction; it's part of the culture. Everybody contributes a little to the thing that makes the room worth being in.
