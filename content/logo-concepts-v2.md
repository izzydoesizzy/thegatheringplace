# The Gathering Place — Logo Concepts, Volume 2

20 additional concepts, all rooted in what made the Hearthstone work: geometric arc-based abstraction, dual/triple readings in a single mark, warmth and community in clean minimal form.

**What the Hearthstone does well:** Three overlapping arcs share a base point — your eye reads it simultaneously as a flame AND as three people leaning in toward each other. Neither reading cancels the other. That's the goal for each of these.

---

## Concept 11: The Embrace

Two large arcs curving toward each other from opposite sides of the composition — concave inward — nearly encircling a small, bright central dot but leaving a deliberate gap at the top. Like arms held open in welcome. Like parentheses around something rare. The gap is the invitation. The dot at center is the fire, the gathering, the thing being protected.

- **Dual reading**: Open arms / a sacred space being held
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} Two large symmetrical curved arcs facing each other from opposite sides, concave inward, nearly encircling a small bright gold dot at center but with a deliberate gap at top, like open arms or parentheses embracing a precious point of light. Warm gold on deep charcoal. Minimal, geometric, balanced.`

---

## Concept 12: The Vessel

Two symmetrical arcs meeting at a sharp base point and opening upward and outward — the classic chalice or cup form. Rendered in clean gold linework, no fill. Like a cupped hand offering something. Like a vessel waiting to be filled. The interior of the vessel is dark charcoal space — the emptiness is intentional, the space where something will be placed.

- **Dual reading**: A cup / cupped hands in offering
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} Two symmetrical curved gold lines meeting at a pointed base and opening outward and upward, forming an elegant chalice or goblet outline shape. No fill inside — just the gold outlines on deep charcoal. Like a cup or cupped hands. Clean line art, minimal, geometric.`

---

## Concept 13: The Canopy

A wide generous arc spanning the full width of the composition, and beneath it — centered, smaller — a matching second arc. Like a great tree canopy sheltering the space below it. Like an arm around a smaller companion. The outer arc is brighter gold; the inner arc slightly dimmer. The space between them is the gathering place itself.

- **Dual reading**: Tree canopy / arm around a shoulder / shelter
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} Two concentric arcs, both opening upward — a larger outer arc spanning the full width, and a smaller inner arc centered beneath it — like a canopy and what shelters beneath it. Warm gold arcs on deep charcoal, the outer arc slightly brighter. Minimal, geometric, protecting.`

---

## Concept 14: The Crescent

Two arcs of slightly different radii, sharing one end point, creating a clean sliver of crescent between them. Not a decorative moon — a geometric one. The crescent shape is the golden element; the surrounding dark is as important as the mark. Like a lit window seen from outside in the dark. Like a sliver of warmth visible through a door left ajar.

- **Dual reading**: Crescent moon / light through a door / window at night
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} A geometric crescent shape formed by two arcs of slightly different radii sharing common endpoints, creating a clean golden sliver. The crescent is solid warm gold on very dark charcoal. Precise, minimal, like a geometric moon or a sliver of light through a door.`

---

## Concept 15: The Double Flame

Two arcs mirrored left-to-right from a shared base point — a bright gold dot — their tops spreading outward and away from each other, like two flames leaning in opposite directions, or like an open book, or like wings spreading. Sibling to the Hearthstone but with a different energy: expansion instead of convergence. Where the Hearthstone pulls inward, this reaches outward.

- **Dual reading**: Two flames / open book / wings
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} Two curved arcs mirrored symmetrically left and right from a shared base point (a small bright gold dot), their tops curving outward and away from each other — like two flames spreading, or open wings, or an open book. Warm gold arcs on deep charcoal. Symmetrical, expanding, elegant.`

---

## Concept 16: The Horizon Rising

A clean thin horizontal gold baseline with three evenly-spaced arcs rising above it — each arc a half-circle, all the same size. Like three flames rising from a hearth. Like three people standing on shared ground. Like three sails on the water. The baseline is the foundation; the three arcs are what emerges from it. Simple, direct, ascending.

- **Dual reading**: Flames rising / people standing / sails
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} A thin horizontal gold line (the baseline) with three identical half-circle arcs rising evenly above it — like three flames, three people, or three sails. All elements warm gold on deep charcoal. Clean, geometric, balanced, ascending.`

---

## Concept 17: The Inverted Hearthstone

The Hearthstone inverted: the shared convergence point is now at the top, and the three arcs spread downward and outward like roots from a common trunk. Or like light from a lantern hanging above. Or like rain from a single cloud. A completely different feeling than the Hearthstone — grounded, spreading, nourishing downward rather than gathering upward.

- **Dual reading**: Roots spreading / hanging light / rain from above
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} Three overlapping curved arcs arranged symmetrically, their tops meeting at a single bright shared point at the apex, their lower portions curving outward and downward — like roots spreading from a trunk, or light hanging from above, or three rivers from one source. Warm gold arcs on deep charcoal. Inverted flame aesthetic.`

## Concept 18: The Leaf

The vesica piscis — two arcs of equal radius, each passing through the center of the other — creates a perfect lens/leaf shape at their intersection. Just those two arcs, no fill, in warm gold. Ancient sacred geometry rendered as a clean modern logo. The lens in the center is where they overlap — the shared space between two things. Simple, timeless, quietly profound.

- **Dual reading**: Leaf / flame / lens / the shared space between two worlds
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} Two curved arcs of equal radius intersecting to create a perfect vesica piscis lens shape — like a leaf or a flame. Just the two clean gold arcs on deep charcoal, no fill. The lens-shaped intersection is the focal point. Precise geometry, minimal, timeless.`

## Concept 19: The Signet

A circle formed by three overlapping arcs — each arc covering roughly one-third of the circle — with the three join-points creating subtle gold-on-gold texture at the intersections. Like a ring divided into three zones of membership. Like a wax seal in three parts. The three sections are not identical colors — the joints are slightly darker, the arcs slightly brighter.

- **Dual reading**: Seal / ring / three-way alliance / membership mark
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} A circular logo mark composed of three overlapping curved arcs that together complete a full circle, each arc covering one-third of the circumference. The three junction points are slightly emphasized. Warm gold on deep charcoal. Like a signet ring or wax seal divided into three membership sections. Clean, geometric, precise.`

## Concept 20: The Seedling

A single arc forming a stem that terminates in a small teardrop or leaf at its tip. Ultra-minimal. Like the first shoot pushing up through soil. Suggests beginning, potential, the thing that will become a forest but right now is just one small brave curve. The entire mark is a single unbroken gold line.

- **Dual reading**: Plant seedling / flame on a stem / a new idea
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} A single continuous gold line forming a gentle curved stem that terminates at its top in a small teardrop or leaf shape — like a seedling emerging, or a candle flame on a thin stem. Ultra-minimal, one continuous line, warm gold on deep charcoal. Delicate but deliberate.`

## Concept 21: The Gateway

Two vertical arcs facing each other — one on the left curving right, one on the right curving left — both concave inward. The space between them is the gateway, the passage, the doorway. You step through the negative space between the arcs. The arcs themselves are in warm gold; the gap between them is dark charcoal — the darkness of what lies ahead, not yet known.

- **Dual reading**: An open gateway / two people facing each other / parentheses
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} Two vertical curved arcs facing each other — concave inward — creating an open gateway shape between them. The space between the arcs is the passage. Both arcs are warm gold on deep charcoal. Like pillars of a gate, or two people facing each other, or a parenthesis surrounding empty space.`

## Concept 22: The Ripple

Three half-circle arcs, all sharing the same center point at the bottom of the composition, each progressively larger — like ripples spreading outward from a single stone dropped in still water. Only the top halves of the circles are visible; the composition is cropped at the center point. Radiating, calm, expanding outward from one moment.

- **Dual reading**: Water ripples / sound waves / expanding impact
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} Three concentric half-circle arcs all sharing the same center point at the bottom of the composition, each one progressively larger — like ripples in water or sound waves radiating outward. Only the upper half-circles are shown. Warm gold arcs on deep charcoal. Radiating, expanding, calm.`

## Concept 23: The Orbit

A small, bright central dot — the fire, the gathering — with a single elliptical arc sweeping around it. Not a complete ellipse; it breaks off before completing its circuit — a deliberate gap. The arc is in motion, never quite closing. Like a moon that always comes back. Like someone who found their orbit and keeps returning.

- **Dual reading**: Orbit / a comet / always returning
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} A small bright gold dot at center with a single elliptical arc sweeping around it, the arc stopping before completing a full circuit — a deliberate gap. The arc suggests motion, orbit, return. Warm gold on deep charcoal. Minimal, dynamic, like a planet's path or a comet's approach.`

---

## Concept 24: The Parenthesis

Two arcs facing each other — concave inward — but positioned far apart, with a wide expanse of dark charcoal between them. The mark IS the space between the parentheses. Whatever goes inside is set apart, made special, held with care. The gold arcs are the frame; the darkness is the content. Unusual for a logo: the negative space is the point.

- **Dual reading**: Parentheses (what's inside is special) / open arms / two people at either end of a conversation
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} Two matching curved arcs positioned far apart on opposite sides of the composition, both concave and facing inward — like a wide pair of parentheses. The large dark charcoal space between them is deliberately prominent. Warm gold arcs on deep charcoal. The space between is the subject.`

## Concept 25: The Triad Point

Three thin arcs, each extending from the same bright central point at 120° angles — like a peace symbol without its enclosing circle, or like three people all touching hands at the center. Each arc curves gently. The central meeting point is the brightest element in the composition. Triadic, balanced, convergent.

- **Dual reading**: Three joining hands / peace symbol / three paths from one source
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} Three curved arcs meeting at a single bright central point, extending outward at 120-degree angles — like a peace symbol without the circle, or three people touching hands at center. Each arc curves gently. The central meeting point is brighter gold. Warm gold on deep charcoal. Triadic, balanced, convergent.`

## Concept 26: The Waxing Stack

Three crescent shapes, each slightly larger than the last, stacked concentrically and slightly offset upward — like three phases of a waxing moon. The smallest crescent nestles inside the curve of the medium one, which nestles inside the largest. Suggests gathering, accumulation, building toward fullness. Something growing week by week.

- **Dual reading**: Moon phases / growth / things building toward something
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} Three crescent shapes of increasing size, concentrically stacked and slightly offset — each one a phase of waxing toward fullness — the smallest nested inside the medium, the medium nested inside the largest. Warm gold crescents on deep charcoal. Like moon phases or accumulated growth.`

## Concept 27: The Comet Arc

A single sweeping arc that thickens and brightens at its leading edge and tapers to nearly nothing at its trailing edge — like a comet in motion, or a conductor's baton at the apex of a stroke, or the moment before something arrives. The sense of motion is the mark. Direction, velocity, arrival.

- **Dual reading**: Comet / moment of arrival / a stroke of a pen
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} A single sweeping arc that is thicker and brighter gold at its leading end and tapers to a fine point at its trailing end — like a comet, a brushstroke, or a conductor's arc. Warm gold on deep charcoal. Suggests motion, direction, the moment before arrival.`

## Concept 28: The Three Notes

Three small identical arc shapes — like the curved head of a musical note without stem or flag — staggered diagonally across the composition, slightly ascending from lower-left to upper-right. Like three ideas hanging in the air of a room. Like the moment after someone says something true and it floats there. Light, airy, suggesting something heard.

- **Dual reading**: Musical notes / three ideas floating / the echo of a good conversation
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} Three small identical curved arc shapes (like musical note heads without stems), staggered diagonally from lower-left to upper-right, ascending. Like three musical notes or three ideas floating in air. Warm gold on deep charcoal. Light, playful, yet refined. Minimal and floating.`

---

## Concept 29: The Triskelion

Three identical curved arcs rotating from a shared central point, each arc sweeping clockwise — like a triskelion or triple spiral, but geometric rather than Celtic. Each arm is a single clean curve. The rotation suggests movement, energy, the constant return of members to the space. Ancient symbol rendered contemporary.

- **Dual reading**: Triskelion / three in motion / constant return
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} Three identical curved arcs radiating from a shared central point and sweeping clockwise — like a modern geometric triskelion or triple spiral. Each arm is a single clean curve of equal weight. Warm gold on deep charcoal. Rotating, dynamic, suggesting perpetual return and renewal.`

---

## Concept 30: The Aperture

Six thin gold arcs arranged in a circle, each one slightly curved, their inner ends meeting at center and outer ends at the circumference — like a camera aperture opening, or like an iris. The blades of the aperture overlap slightly. Suggests: focus, the act of letting light in, choosing what enters. A controlled opening to the right things.

- **Dual reading**: Camera aperture / iris / a controlled opening / curation
- **Prompt**: `${BASE} Six thin curved gold arcs arranged in a circular aperture or iris pattern — each arc overlapping slightly, inner ends meeting at center, outer ends at circumference. Like a camera aperture or eye iris. Warm gold on deep charcoal. Precise, geometric, suggesting focus and curated openness.`
